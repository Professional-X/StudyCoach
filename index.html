What is StudyCoach?

StudyCoach is a free, open-source AI study management app that combines 8 powerful tools into one distraction-free interface. Whether you're preparing for exams, learning a new skill, or organizing your coursework, StudyCoach helps you study smarter using evidence-based techniques like spaced repetition (SM-2 algorithm) and active recall.

Unlike other study apps, StudyCoach requires no account, stores all data locally in your browser, and works completely offline. Your study data never leaves your device.

All Features — One App

AI Study Planner

Auto-generate weekly study schedules from your subjects, deadlines, and weak areas. One click to a fully optimized week.

Spaced Repetition Flashcards

Create decks manually or let AI generate cards. SM-2 algorithm schedules reviews at optimal intervals for maximum retention.

Pomodoro Focus Timer

Presets for 25/5, 50/10, 90/20 sessions. Ambient soundscapes, keyboard shortcuts, and post-session reflection logging.

AI Summarizer

Paste any text and get structured summaries, key points, practice questions, and a glossary of key terms instantly.

Dashboard & Streaks

GitHub-style activity heatmap, daily streaks, weekly goals, time charts by subject, and AI recommendations on what to study next.

Goals & Milestones

Set weekly, semester, or exam goals. AI breaks ambitious goals into actionable weekly sub-goals with progress tracking.

Resource Library

Upload PDFs, images, save links and notes. Tag by subject and topic. Everything syncs to flashcard decks.

Subject Organization

Hierarchical structure: Subject, Topic, Resources & Cards. Progress bars, difficulty ratings, color-coded.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is StudyCoach?

StudyCoach is a free, open-source AI study app that combines 8 tools: AI study planner, spaced repetition flashcards, Pomodoro focus timer, AI summarizer, goal tracking, resource library, and activity dashboard. No login required.

Is StudyCoach free?

Yes, completely free and open source under the MIT license. No premium features, no subscriptions, no ads.

Does StudyCoach work offline?

Yes. All data is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. Core features including flashcards, planner, and focus timer work without internet.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Just open the app and start studying. Your data stays on your device. No sign-up, no email, no password.

What is spaced repetition?

StudyCoach uses the SM-2 algorithm to schedule flashcard reviews at optimal intervals. Hard cards appear more frequently, easy cards less often — scientifically proven to maximize long-term retention.

Can StudyCoach generate flashcards with AI?

Yes. Enter any topic and the AI auto-generates relevant question-answer flashcard decks for you to study.
